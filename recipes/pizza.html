It' Pizza time!

Ingredients for making a great pizza!

  • 5 cups Double Zero Flour, All Purpose Flour or Bread Flour ( add additional flour if the dough is very sticky)
  • 2 & 1/2 teaspoons active dry yeast
  • 3 cups water ( you can start with 2 &3/4 cup and add additional water if you need to)
  • 2 teaspoons salt
  • 1 tablespoon organic extra virgin olive oil

Instructions for making a great pizza!

  1. In a small bowl, add 1/2 cup of warm water and the active dry yeast. Set the yeast aside for 5 minutes until it gets foamy. In a large bowl, mix all the ingredients together and knead the dough until when poked, the dough springs back. Add additional flour if the dough is to sticky!
  2. Set the dough aside in a warm place covered completely with a tea towel or plate.
  3. Allow the dough to rise until double size about 5-7 hours (If you have time, I would allow the dough to rise overnight in the fridge)
  4. When the dough has double in size. Punch the dough until it deflates place in a floured clean surface. Cut the dough in 4 or 5 parts.
  5. Knead each dough ball for 1 minute and place on a floured rectangle baking sheet and cover with a tea towel. Set the dough aside and allow for another rise until the dough doubles in size about 1-2 hours.
  6. Once the doughs doubled in size, preheat oven to the highest 500 tp 550 degrees. During this time, you can prepare all your pizza toppings and sauce.
  7. When ready to bake, place the pizza stone in the oven and allow to heat up. Roll out the dough thinly on a floured pizza peel and add your sauce and toppings.
  8. Using the pizza peel, slide the pizza onto the pizza stone in the oven and allow to bake for 7-10 minutes until it bakes to your desired crispness. Enjoy the pizza warm!
